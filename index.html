Editorial Photography at Falmouth University, UK. Class of 2019.

Since graduating, I have consistently been shooting both Portrait and Music events with as freelance work alongside my fulltime job. My passion lies in creative endeavours and I have always sought out different mediums that allow me to follow this; during my time studying I was heavily involved in promoting and organsing nightlife events via a start-up collective. Upon graduating I've been living in London and photographing a range of portrait subjects and music events / artists - though sadly it has remained a side job and passion venture for a number of years. But now, in the early months of learning to program and build websites and apps I am excited to apply my skills and background to future creative projects and work be it personal or professional.
